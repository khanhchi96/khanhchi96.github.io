The Audi A6 Sedan

From every perspective. From the widened single frame, the side air intakes with sporty chrome trims, to the narrow headlights – the progressive nature A6 Sedan front gives a great impression at first sight. The sharpened shoulder line with strong curves above the wheel arches accentuates the quattro inheritance. The exterior mirrors have a sporty location by the armrest. Moreover, the coupé-like flowing roofline elegantly leads into the rear end, formed in three dimensions with a characteristic aluminium style.

Fuel consumption combined *: 6.8-4.3 l/100km
CO₂-emissions combined *: 154-112 g/km
